What We Offer

Two disciplines.
One passion.

Discipline 01

Windsurfing

The original adrenaline-fuelled ride. We coach windsurfing from your very first session right through to advanced wave and freestyle techniques.

Absolute beginners — first board time & basic rigging
Improvers — steering, beach starts & jibing
Intermediate — harness use, planning & footstraps
Advanced — wave riding, jumps & advanced manoeuvres
WIND

Discipline 02

Wing Foiling

The sport taking the world by storm. Wing foiling combines a handheld inflatable wing with a hydrofoil board — once you're flying above the water, there's nothing quite like it.

Introduction — wing handling on land & in water
First flights — body dragging & board control
Foiling — take-off technique & direction changes
Progression — tacks, jibes & building confidence
